About Herbalife

Inspiring Healthy Living Around the World

Our History

Herbalife was founded in 1980 with a mission to improve the nutritional habits of the world. Starting from a single idea and a handful of products, Herbalife has grown into a global leader in nutrition, operating in over 90 countries with millions of independent distributors.

Our Mission

Our mission is to change people's lives by providing the best nutrition and weight-management products in the world, backed by scientific research and personalized coaching. We are dedicated to empowering people to live healthier, more active lives.

Our Team

Herbalife’s strength lies in its passionate community of distributors, dedicated employees, and leading experts in health, nutrition, and fitness. Our team works together to deliver personalized support and promote wellness through education and innovation.
